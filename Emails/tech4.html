Stratix 10 FPGAs
Available Today

The industry’s first 14nm FPGA, Stratix 10, is shipping to customers and ready to order.  Stratix 10 is uniquely positioned to exceed the performance demands of high-end compute and data-intensive applications ranging from data centers, communications infrastructure, cloud computing, radar and imaging systems.

The need for more bandwidth and lower latency in our networks, flexibility in our data centers to react to new and changing workloads, and managing performance per watt are all key drivers for Stratix 10.  Furthermore, as system demands or hardware requirements change, Stratix 10 can be reconfigured in milliseconds to accommodate your needs.

A critical part of Intel’s growth strategy, Stratix 10 is leading with these unprecedented capabilities:

  • 2X the core performance and over 5X the density compared to previous generation devices
  • Up to 70 percent lower power than Stratix V FPGAs for equivalent performance
  • Up to 10 TFLOPS of single-precision floating point DSP performance
  • Integrated High-Bandwidth Memory (HBM2) in-package options
  • Embedded quad-core 64-bit ARM Cortex-A53 processor
