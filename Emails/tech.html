Data Centers are Running Short on Compute Capacity

More than 50 billion devices will be connected to the internet by the year 2020.  And more than 200 billion sensors will be web enabled in the coming years. One of the biggest challenges in IT today is getting enough compute resources to handle current processing tasks and keep up with future needs.

Read the special report from Inside HPC to learn about:
  • The gap between what is available have today and what is needed in the future
  • Finding the compute horsepower to run increasingly larger analytical models
  • How enterprises can best slice and dice Big Data without adding servers
